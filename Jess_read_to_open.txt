It is complicated to open slowveld. 
Follow instructions below step by step.


1. Open vscode 
2. click control + ` - this will open a command line`(terminal) at the bottom of the interface
3. on the top right of the terminal, it will say powershell, then a '+'then a drop down. Click on the dropdown
4. from the drop down click "command prompt"
5. Delete the powershell terminal
6. In the Command prompt click D:
7. copy/paste --> cd D:\Documents\slowveld
8. copy/paste --> .\.venv\Scripts\activate
9. copy/paste --> python manage.py runserver
10: open chrom broswer in icognito mode
11. add url --> http://127.0.0.1:8000/
12. Log into your admin account to start using the inventory management system
